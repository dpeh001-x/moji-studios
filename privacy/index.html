Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Moji Studios ("we", "us", "our") runs moji-studios.com. This page explains what data we collect when you visit, why we collect it, and what we do with it. We try to keep things minimal and friendly — no creepy tracking, no selling your stuff. Promise.

What we collect

When you visit moji-studios.com, we automatically collect a small amount of data:

  • Standard server logs from Cloudflare (IP address, browser, country, time of visit) — used to keep the site fast and to block abuse.
  • Aggregate analytics data — pages visited, screen size, referrer. No personal data.
  • Anything you voluntarily send through the contact form (name, email, message).

Cookies & tracking

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential — required for the site to function (e.g. remembering your cookie-consent choice).
  • Analytics — to understand which pages people visit. No personally identifiable data is sent to us.
  • Advertising — Google AdSense uses cookies to display ads. These cookies may be used to personalize ads based on your previous visits to this and other sites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted services to run the site:

  • Google AdSense — advertising · privacy policy
  • Google Workspace (Gmail) — for our admin@ inbox · privacy policy
  • GitHub — source code hosting · privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — typography · privacy policy

How we use your data

  • To keep the site secure and fast.
  • To respond when you email us or submit the contact form.
  • To understand which pages people enjoy (and which to improve).
  • To display relevant advertising via Google AdSense.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with anyone except the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent needed to operate this site.

Your rights

If you live in the EU, UK, California, or anywhere with comparable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete it.
  • Object to or restrict its processing.
  • Withdraw consent for cookies (clear your cookies and our banner will reappear).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@moji-studios.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time — usually because we add a new third-party service or change how something works. We'll bump the "Last updated" date at the top whenever we do, and significant changes will be announced on the site.
