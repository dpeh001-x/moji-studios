★ WHY MOJI ★

We make the games we wish we still played.

We grew up on Super Mario, MapleStory, Pokémon, Neopets, Harvest Moon — the golden age when games felt like places. Moji Studios is our love letter to that era, rebuilt with modern engines, hand-drawn pixel art, and obsessive attention to feel.
